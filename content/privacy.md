## What we collect

- **Cert numbers you submit** and the resulting scan analysis. Stored against your account (if signed in) or anonymously (if not).
- **A hash of your IP address** when you use the free tier without signing up, solely to enforce the 1 scan / day limit. Rotated regularly.
- **Sign-in info** from your Google account (or email/password) when you create an account.
- **Stripe billing data** for paying customers. We don't see your card number — Stripe handles that.

## What we don't do

- We don't sell your data.
- We don't share your scan history with grading companies.
- We don't fingerprint your browser beyond what's required for login sessions.
